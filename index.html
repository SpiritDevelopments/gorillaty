ABOUT

We are the Revolutionary Army [Gorillaty First] working to rescue allies from all zoos on this planet. We emigrated to this planet long before humans were born, but they despise our former apes as apes. Many times wars broke out between them and us, but in the face of their armed forces, they couldn't stand. Nowadays, friends are being caught in zoos and other places, and they are shown so that they will never fight against them.

Is it okay to abandon my friends as they are? Tarzan is a dream story. We stand up and make this planet a monkey planet again.
